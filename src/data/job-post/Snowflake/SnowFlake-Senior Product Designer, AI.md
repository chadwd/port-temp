# Senior Product Designer, AI \- Snowflake

Snowflake is about empowering enterprises to achieve their full potential — and people too. With a culture that’s all in on impact, innovation, and collaboration, Snowflake is the sweet spot for building big, moving fast, and taking technology — and careers — to the next level.

Build the future of Business Intelligence Agents, join the Snowflake team.

Snowflake Design is a world-class team of curious problem solvers on a mission to evolve and improve the way the world works with data. Great design is a strategic differentiator for Snowflake — but that differentiation doesn’t come automatically. We’re looking for talented, collaborative, and technical designers to join the team and drive the mission forward while doing career-defining work.

On the Snowflake AI team, Product Designers identify opportunities, shape the vision of our products, and ensure we’re delivering highly crafted, honed products that our data analysts, data engineers, and data stewards love. As we continue to develop new products and push our existing products forward, we’re looking for Designers to redefine the future of working with AI and data. Product Designers at Snowflake work on a variety of surfaces like Snowflake Intelligence, agent administration, semantic views, observability, and more.

### **YOUR IMPACT:**

* Drive a significant, complex, and technical product area forward, shaping vision and owning execution  
* Delight customers by designing high-quality AI-forward products that deliver meaningful value and protect and govern sensitive data.  
* Champion user empathy in everything you do, evangelizing partners to do the same


### **YOUR ROLE:**

* Identify and drive design opportunities by connecting the dots between user and business needs  
* Collaborate and effectively influence your engineering and product partners to design and deliver excellent products  
* Work end-to-end by identifying opportunities, establishing the vision, and working with engineering to deliver high quality, robust solutions  
* Collaborate with the broader design team to ensure a seamless product experience  
* Partner with researchers, data scientists, and product managers to drive solutions by leveraging qualitative and quantitative customer insights  
* Utilize and extend Snowflake’s Design System  
* Develop strong rationale for design decisions, and articulate your perspective  
* Apply systems thinking to navigate and solve complex problems  
* Stay abreast of the latest in AI and incorporate the capabilities into the user experience\`


### **YOUR EXPERIENCE:**

* 6+ years as a product designer  
* Experience with AI and Code  
* Experience collaborating closely with engineering and product partners  
* Experience working with data and technical complexity  
* End-to-end ownership of meaningful features or surfaces  
* Your end-user advocacy has shaped product experiences you can point to  
* Shipped products that have simplified complex workflows  
* You have iterated on shipped products based on customer usage and feedback  
* Lead product features or surfaces and balanced long term strategy with short term execution  
* Experience communicating your ideas both upwards to stakeholders and across to cross-functional team members  
* Experience designing for both Consumer and Enterprise products


### **NICE TO HAVE:**

* Experience in fast-paced environments with quick delivery and iteration cycles  
* Ability to work autonomously or with engineering partners to get out of mockups faster  
* Interest in the data space and cutting edge technologies  
* Experience with web technologies like HTML, Javascript/React, and CSS
